About Run-A-Thonia

Looking for Best Products?

We, Run-A-Thonia, are fast-growing B2B and B2C e-commerce startup, Run-A-Thonia is an online buying platform for multi-category consumer products focused on the new ‘WhatsApp first’ India. Our mission is to target the Retailers and Direct to Consumers from metro and non-metro cities and rural market spaces who haven’t shopped enough online and are uncomfortable on other platforms. These users are primarily on mobile & social media. Run-A-Thonia makes it extremely easy for these users to buy in a couple of clicks or via assigned Key Account Manager with a peace of mind. Further, it allows the users to share deals with their friends and avail discounts if their friends purchase the products via the share link. It is a new retail model in which customers get products at cheaper than wholesale rates. The idea is around demand aggregation (C2B) of mass-market products using social virality, thereby significantly reducing operational costs. Our product catalogue includes Battery, Inverter and Solar products, Electrical and Appliances, Safety, Industrial, Office Supplies, home décor etc. Come and build Run-A-Thonia For India, and challenge yourself in the process.
